Conduct committee — incident scoring rubric

Section 1: boolean escalators override everything. Section 2: scored factors (0–5), with severity overrides for high scores.
Section 1 — Automatic escalators
Any one checked = minimum Elevated response. Two or more = Severe. These override the scored total.
Section 2 — Scored factors
One score per factor (0–5). Any single factor at 5 = minimum Elevated. Any factor at 4 = minimum Moderate. Two or more at 4+ = minimum Elevated.
