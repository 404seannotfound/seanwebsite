Burning Man Principles Indicator (BMPI) Quiz

What is the BMPI?

The Burning Man Principles Indicator (BMPI) is a personality assessment that helps you understand how you align with the ten core principles of Burning Man culture. These principles, developed by Burning Man founder Larry Harvey, represent the essential values and behaviors that guide the Burning Man community.

This quiz will:

  • Present you with 50 real-world scenarios
  • Measure your natural responses to these situations
  • Calculate which principles resonate most strongly with your values
  • Provide insights into your primary principle and how it shapes your interactions

There are no right or wrong answers - this is about understanding your natural inclinations and how they align with these transformative principles.
